About Me

Where light meets code

Hi, I'm Pasan Wijaya Wickrama — a passionate photographer and cinematic web developer. I blend the artistry of photography with the craft of cinematic-quality frontend development to create experiences that feel alive.

On my YouTube channel, I document the process — from scouting locations and composing shots, to building visually stunning websites that move and breathe. My goal is to inspire creators to see both disciplines as one unified art form.
